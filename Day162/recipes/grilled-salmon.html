Grilled Salmon with Lemon Butter

25 mins 4 servings Easy
Seafood Healthy Quick

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets (6 oz each)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Instructions

  1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat (about 375-400Â°F).
  2. Pat salmon fillets dry with paper towels. Brush both sides with olive oil.
  3. In a small bowl, mix salt, pepper, minced garlic, and oregano. Sprinkle this mixture evenly over the salmon fillets.
  4. Place salmon on the grill, skin-side down if your fillets have skin. Close the grill lid and cook for 4-5 minutes.
  5. Carefully flip the salmon using a spatula. Cook for another 3-4 minutes until the salmon flakes easily with a fork.
  6. While salmon cooks, melt butter in a small saucepan over low heat. Stir in lemon juice and parsley.
  7. Remove salmon from grill and drizzle with the lemon butter sauce. Serve immediately with lemon wedges.

Nutrition Information

Per serving: 320 calories, 22g fat, 1g carbohydrates, 28g protein
